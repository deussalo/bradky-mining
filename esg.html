Low-Carbon Innovation

Waste-to-Energy via Inrim Technology

A defining differentiator for BRADKY Mining is the integration of Waste-to-Energy technology via Inrim Waste to Energy Pty Ltd. Continuous pyrolysis converts site and community waste streams into clean electricity and EN590-grade synthetic diesel on site, at scale, with zero net GHG emissions.

How Pyrolysis Works

♻

Waste Feedstock

Site waste, plastics, biomass and community refuse collected as input fuel

🔥

Thermal Conversion

Continuous pyrolysis at 500–700°C in an oxygen-depleted environment

💨

Syngas Production

Hydrocarbon syngas extracted and separated for further conversion

⚡

Power Generation

Self-sustaining electricity for all site operations

⛽

EN590 Diesel

Synthetic diesel to European standard for mining fleet

Technology Specifications

  • Continuous pyrolysis waste processing — not batch
  • Operating temperatures: 500–700°C
  • Zero greenhouse gas emissions from conversion process
  • Self-sustaining energy generation — no external fuel input required
  • Produces electricity and EN590-grade synthetic diesel
  • Minimal char by-product from process

Strategic Benefits to BRADKY Operations

  • Reduced reliance on grid power and diesel imports
  • Lower operating costs and improved AISC
  • Improved ESG performance and investor reporting metrics
  • Positive social outcomes — waste reduction in host communities
  • Local employment creation in waste management
  • Potential carbon credit generation and trading revenue

Health & Safety
