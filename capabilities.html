Capabilities

Full-scope, integrated mining capability

Two complementary business lines, ownership & operation and advisory, deliver aligned execution from acquisition to optimised steady-state production.

Business Line A

Asset Ownership & Operation

BRADKY acquires, develops and directly operates gold mining assets. The owner-operator model ensures full alignment between capital, strategy and daily execution.

Mine Development

  • Greenfield and brownfield development
  • Mine design, scheduling and production planning
  • Contractor selection and mobilisation
  • Commissioning and production ramp-up

Operational Management

  • Open-pit and underground mining operations
  • Drill and blast, load and haul optimisation
  • Workforce, contractor and safety management
  • Grade control and reconciliation
  • Cost control and productivity optimisation

Processing & Metallurgy

  • Crushing, milling and CIL/CIP operations
  • Recovery optimisation and metallurgical testing
  • Throughput expansion and debottlenecking
  • Reagent, consumables and maintenance optimisation

Asset Optimisation

  • Continuous improvement systems
  • Life-of-mine extension strategies
  • Capital efficiency and margin enhancement
  • Technology integration
Business Line B

Mining Management & Advisory

Technical, commercial and operational advisory supports the full transaction cycle, from pre-deal diligence through to stabilised post-acquisition operations.

M&A Due Diligence

  • Resource and reserve quality assessment (JORC-aligned)
  • Grade distribution, metallurgy and recoveries
  • Mining method suitability and strip ratios
  • Process plant condition and throughput evaluation
  • Infrastructure, logistics and power supply

Risk & Jurisdictional Assessment

  • Jurisdictional, permitting and sovereign risk
  • ESG, social licence and legacy issue review
  • Capital intensity and operating cost structure

Feasibility & Valuation

  • Pre-feasibility studies (PSF) and definitive studies (DSF)
  • DCF, NPV and comparable company valuations
  • Financial modelling and sensitivity analysis

Post-Acquisition & Recovery

  • Project management and operations reporting
  • Mine planning and process plant optimisation
  • Grade and recovery improvement
  • Project recovery and turnaround

Due Diligence

A bank-grade, institutional due diligence framework is applied to every acquisition, covering resource quality, technical risk, jurisdictional exposure and valuation.

Due Diligence Framework

Bank-grade assessment, every acquisition

Only assets that demonstrate robust economics under conservative pricing assumptions progress to acquisition.

  • Resource and reserve quality (JORC-aligned analysis)
  • Grade distribution and geological confidence
  • Continuity and structural complexity assessment
  • Metallurgical testwork and recovery benchmarks
  • Process plant condition and throughput capacity
  • Reagent and consumables cost assessment
  • Mining method suitability and strip ratio analysis
  • Infrastructure, logistics and power supply security
  • Capital intensity and sustaining capex requirements
  • Permitting status, tenure and renewal risk
  • Sovereign risk and regulatory environment
  • ESG, social licence and legacy community issues
  • Multiple valuation methodologies: DCF, NPV, comparable transactions
  • Operating cost structure and margin sensitivity
  • Downside scenario testing at conservative gold prices
